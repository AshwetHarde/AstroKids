About Us

Explore the Universe with AstroKids: Where Learning Meets Adventure

We Are Spacewalk Edu.

In the future, AstroKids aims to be the best place for kids to learn about space. We want to make learning about space fun and exciting by using cool technology and making lessons that fit each kid's interests. We also want kids from all over the world to connect and share their love for space.

AstroKids was launched with a specific focus on underprivileged children, marking the beginning of our journey in India and aspiring to extend our impact globally. Our core mission is to provide enriching experiences for children from diverse backgrounds, offering them opportunities for exploration through immersive virtual space adventures.We launched AstroKids with a focus on underprivileged children, originating in India and with a vision to extend our impact globally.
